Quick gates · this page
One or two yes/no checks that resolve the decision immediately. Most of Miso's daily work lives here.

These aren't lesser rules — they're just the ones that don't need a full tree. A kitten-desex check, a peak-hour slot filter, a pickup-text trigger. Miso fires through them dozens of times a day, usually without anyone noticing. Compared to the deep cascades, these are the ones that should just work and stay out of your way.
